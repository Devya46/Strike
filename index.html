Who founded Strike?

Rohit Srivastava, the founder and market strategist of Indiacharts.com, is the driving force behind Strike. Rohit has over 30 years of experience in understanding market trends and making the most of them. Rohit, with Strike, brings his extensive experience and expertise in stock market trading and analysis to a wider audience, revolutionizing the way investors approach the stock market.

When is Strike founded?

Strike was founded in 2023 as an all-in-one stock market analytical & research platform.

Where is the Strike Headquarters?

Strike operates primarily in India with a global reach. Headquarters located in Mumbai, India.

Who are the Researchers in Strike?

Strike houses a dedicated team of market analysts, data scientists, and SEBI-registered research analysts providing institutional-grade insights.

Strike provides advanced charting tools, pattern recognition, indicators, and real-time data to help traders spot trends and execute trades confidently.
