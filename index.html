Our Founder

Rohit Srivastava, the founder and market strategist of Indiacharts.com, is the driving force behind Strike. With over 30 years of experience in understanding market trends, he brings deep expertise in stock market trading and analysis — revolutionising the way investors approach the stock market.
A seasoned market strategist and founder of Strike and Indiacharts, he has over 18 years of experience managing funds at Sharekhan. He has a deep understanding of market trends and excels at forecasting market movements.
He began his journey in 1990, independently mastering core principles like Dow Theory, momentum trading, and Elliott Wave analysis. He actively shares his insights through his blog, Indiacharts.
At Sharekhan Ltd., he held key roles including Market Analyst, Head of Research, and Market Strategist. He developed the Value Wave model, launched PMS based on absolute returns, and authored the Indian Economic Winter Report (2010) mapping India's Kondratieff economic cycle.
