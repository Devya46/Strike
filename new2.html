Our Founder

Rohit Srivastava, the founder and market strategist of Indiacharts.com, is the driving force behind Strike. Rohit has over 30 years of experience in understanding market trends and making the most of them. Rohit, with Strike, brings his extensive experience and expertise in stock market trading and analysis to a wider audience, revolutionising the way investors approach the stock market.
Rohit Srivastava, a seasoned market strategist and founder of Strike and Indiacharts, provides stock market insights and analysis. With over 18 years of experience managing funds at Sharekhan, he has a deep understanding of market trends and excels at forecasting market movements.
Srivastava began his journey in 1990, independently mastering the fundamentals of stock market analysis, including Dow theory, momentum trading, and Eliot wave analysis. He is dedicated to sharing his knowledge, regularly contributing to his blog, 'Indiacharts.'
At Sharekhan Ltd., he held various roles, including market analyst, Head of Research, and Market Strategist. His innovative approach led to the development of the Value Wave model, a novel approach to stock research, and the launch of the first Portfolio Management Services based on absolute returns. He also authored the Indian Economic Winter Report in 2010, which mapped India's Kondratieff economic cycle.
